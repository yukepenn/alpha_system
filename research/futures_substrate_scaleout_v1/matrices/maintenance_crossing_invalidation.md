## Audit Notes

- `fixed_horizon`: the read-only counter classifies candidate windows whose horizon would cross the maintenance / trade-date break even when the exact terminal row is absent; those candidates are invalidated rather than emitted as measured returns.
- `session_close_maintenance_flat`: close-out labels use same-contract last-bar-at-or-before-break semantics. The `Close-out boundary dropped` column records windows bounded at the break; `Dropped` remains zero for this family because no forward measurement crosses the break.
- `cost_adjusted`: counts are generated read-only from canonical BBO timestamps and reported on the emitted label-version surface across the two cost labels.
- `2018`: omitted from all matrices because the campaign acceptance inventory marks 2018 blocked and the P16-P20 summaries exclude it.

## Hard stops (escalate to user, do not auto-proceed)

New paid data / feed (incl. onboarding `fomc`/`cpi` cards — record-only, never sourced); broker / live /
paper / order / capital; introducing a new instrument or universe; non-regenerable deletion (incl. retiring
the Track-A cards before the canonical line subsumes them with a parity test); committing raw / canonical /
Parquet / SQLite / secrets / local registries or any `runs/` path; weakening or bypassing any truth-chain /
no-lookahead / no-second-value-truth / surrogate-FDR `ZERO_PASS_MET` / family-budget / exploratory-no-promote
invariant; editing the frozen `MechanismCard`/`SetupSpec`/`AlphaSpec`/`StudySpec` schemas (re-hashes locked IDs);
touching any FUTSUB or DIFFERENTIATED_KILLSHOT_V1 STOPPED-campaign artifact or producer; building any downstream
module (Mining V2, FactorLibrary, AlphaBook, Strategy Sandbox, PA grammar, sequence/geometry sweep) or a major
Compass-level route change; promoting any exploratory readout or survivor (a survivor is SURFACED for the
reviewer-gated survivor-gate decision, never auto-promoted).
